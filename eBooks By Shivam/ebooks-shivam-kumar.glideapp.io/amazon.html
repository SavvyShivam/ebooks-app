Amazon Prime and Prime Video

Amazon Prime is a subscription-based service that gives you various benefits like faster delivery, special discounts and early access to Amazon's promotions and sales. Amazon Prime Video is an Over The Top (OTT) service which brings you a plethora of video content including new releases and Amazon's original shows for only Rs. 129 per month or Rs. 999 per year. You can watch unlimited movies, television shows and Exclusive Content. In addition to the special early access to the Amazon Great Indian Festival, Amazon Prime customers are also offered the benefit of free shipping.
